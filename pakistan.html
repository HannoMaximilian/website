Pakistan

My travels to Pakistan took place in October 2019. My travels in Iran made me interested in the region, and I ended up going to Pakistan after choosing between other countries. I chose to go to the North of Pakistan, because of the beauty of the landscape and because of the good things that I heard about traveling there. The security situation in the North of Pakistan is very good, and I never felt unsafe walking around alone. There is so much to explore and to see, and the people are extremely friendly. I didn't make very precise travel plans beforehand, and the itineary kind of evolved during the trip. It ended up being: Gilgit, Nanga Parbat, Karimabad and up the Karakoam Highway until Khunjerab Pass, Shimshal, Mount Rakaposhi, Deosai, Mastuj, Swat Valley, Peschawar, Lahore and Islamabad.
