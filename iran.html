Iran

My travels in Iran took place in March 2018. Traveling in Iran without a planned itineary was very easy. Since there were a few places I knew I wanted to visit (Isfahan, Persepolis, Teheran) a very general route was given. Everything else evolved after speaking to Iranians on the way. The persian hospitality is unbelievable and sometimes even made me a little uncomforable. During my entire trip, which also led to somme very untouristic places, I felt very safe and people would go out of their way to help me. The places I visited were Teheran, Kermanshah, Sanandaj, Isfahan, Varzaneh, Yazd, Shiraz, Lorestan region and Zandschan. Communication was not to hard, since many Iranians speak English, and some even speak some German. I will definetly return to Iran when I have the chance, since there is so much to see and do.
